## 1. The Myth

Ragnarök is not just destruction — it is **renewal**. After the old world burns, a new world rises from the waters. Líf and Lífþrasir survive hidden in Hoddmímis holt. Baldr returns from Hel. The world begins again.

## 2. System Reset Philosophy

A complete system reset is sometimes necessary. But it must be:
1. **Prepared**: Hoddmímis holt — seed data preserved before reset
2. **Controlled**: Not chaos, but a planned transformation
3. **Renewed**: The new system must learn from the old
4. **Honored**: The old system's contribution is acknowledged
