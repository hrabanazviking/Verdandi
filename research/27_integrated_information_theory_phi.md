## 1. Key IIT Concept

Integrated Information Theory measures consciousness as Φ (phi) — the amount of information generated by a system above and beyond the information generated by its parts independently. A system has Φ > 0 when it is more than the sum of its parts.

Freyja's necklace was forged by four dwarves, but its value lies not in the four gems but in the CONNECTIONS between them. This is exactly Φ — the necklace is worth more than the sum of its gems because of how they are connected.
