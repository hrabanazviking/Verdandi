## 1. Wyrd (Fate)

Wyrd is not predetermined fate — it is the pattern that emerges from accumulated actions. Every action adds a layer to örlog (fate-layers), shaping future possibilities.

In VERÐANDI: Every nerve impulse adds a layer to the wyrd buffer. The pattern of past events shapes the probability of future events. This is NOT deterministic scheduling — it is **emergent scheduling** based on accumulated patterns.

## 2. Örlog (Fate-Layers)

Örlog is the accumulated layers of past actions. Each action adds a layer, and the layers compound.

In VERÐANDI: The örlog layer is the commitment delivery system. When an intention is "laid" (scheduled), it becomes a fate-layer that will be delivered at the appropriate time.

## 3. Norns: Urd, Verdandi, Skuld

- **Urd** (Past): The conversation log — what has already happened
- **Verdandi** (Present/Becoming): The nerve hub — what is happening now
- **Skuld** (Future/Debt): The prediction engine — what will likely happen next

The three Norns are not separate — they are the same process at different points in the wyrd flow:
- Urd writes to the past (conversation log)
- Verdandi processes the present (nerve hub)
- Skuld predicts the future (pattern prediction)
